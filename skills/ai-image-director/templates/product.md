# Template — Product Photography

Use this template for e-commerce product photography, packshots, hero product images, product detail shots, and product-in-context lifestyle imagery.

---

## When to Use This Template

- E-commerce product images (white background, all-angle)
- Hero product shots for campaign and brand use
- Packshot photography (precise, clean, catalog quality)
- Product detail and texture close-ups
- Product-in-use or product-in-context lifestyle
- Luxury and premium product presentation
- Beauty and skincare product photography

---

## Product Photography Categories

Choose the category that matches the brief:

| Category | Description | Typical Use |
|----------|-------------|-------------|
| **Pure white packshot** | Product on pure white, all details visible | E-commerce, catalog |
| **Hero product** | Single product, dramatic lighting, dark or styled background | Campaign, brand hero |
| **Product detail** | Extreme close-up of specific feature, texture, or material | Product page feature, advertising |
| **Product in context** | Product placed in a relevant environment or with props | Lifestyle, brand editorial |
| **Multiple product** | Several products arranged together | Capsule collection, range shot |
| **Product with model** | Product worn, used, or held by a person | Fashion/beauty campaign |

## Section 1: Product Description

Be precise about every visual element of the product. AI models cannot see your product — they construct it from your words.

### Product Description Template

```
[PRODUCT TYPE]: [FORM AND SHAPE]. 
[PRIMARY MATERIAL AND SURFACE]: [color, finish, texture, reflectivity].
[SECONDARY MATERIALS]: [color, finish, texture].
[LABEL OR PACKAGING]: [material, color, text treatment — no actual text unless Ideogram].
[SIZE IMPRESSION]: [tall/squat/slim/wide — relative to frame].
[DISTINCTIVE FEATURES]: [any unique design element that defines the product].
```

### Surface Property Vocabulary

**Glass**:
- `clear glass, high transparency, visible through`
- `frosted glass, diffuse translucent, no clear reflections`
- `colored glass, semi-opaque, tinted [color]`
- `mirror glass, full reflectivity, environment fully reflected`

**Metal**:
- `polished stainless steel, mirror-like, anisotropic highlight`
- `brushed aluminum, directional grain texture, elongated specular`
- `matte black metal, no specular, absorbs light`
- `gold-tone metal, warm specular, bright highlight`
- `aged brass, warm patina, slight surface variation`

**Plastic / polymer**:
- `high-gloss ABS plastic, strong single specular highlight`
- `matte rubberized coating, no specular, tactile texture`
- `soft-touch polymer, slight sheen, semi-matte`
- `clear acrylic, high transparency, slight blue cast in edges`

**Paper / cardboard**:
- `uncoated matte paper, visible fiber texture, no sheen`
- `coated gloss paper, bright specular highlight`
- `kraft brown cardboard, natural texture, matte`
- `premium embossed paper, raised surface texture, subtle shadow`

**Fabric**:
- `woven textile, visible thread structure, matte`
- `silk fabric, high sheen, liquid highlight behavior`
- `suede leather, matte, directional nap texture`
- `smooth leather, slight gloss, structured crease`

## Section 2: Product Placement and Angle

### Single Product Angles

| Angle | Description | Best Use |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Front facing** | Camera directly in front, product flat | Label visibility, catalog |
| **Three-quarter** | 30–45° rotation from front | Volume, depth, premium feel |
| **Profile / side** | 90° from front | Shape emphasis, slim products |
| **45° top-down** | Camera above at 45°, product below | Flat lay, overhead |
| **Straight top-down** | Camera directly overhead | Flat lay, texture |
| **Low angle hero** | Camera below product looking up | Power, drama, premium |

## Section 4: Lighting for Products

Product lighting must reveal form, texture, and material — not just illuminate the scene.

### White Background (E-Commerce)

```
Even studio lighting, large octaboxes at 45° from both sides, 5500K daylight balanced, 
soft diffused quality, no hard shadows. Background lit separately to pure white. 
No shadows on subject from lighting. Clean catch-light if transparent or reflective material.
```

### Hero / Dark Background (Premium)

```
[SINGLE / TWO SOURCE] lighting setup:
Primary: [SIZE AND TYPE OF MODIFIER] at [DIRECTION], [ANGLE DOWN], [KELVIN]K, [HARD / SOFT].
[FILL — size, direction, intensity relative to key].
[RIM OR ACCENT — if any].
[BACKGROUND LIGHT — separate control of background].
```

### Lighting for Specific Materials

**Glass / transparent product**:
```
Edge lighting: narrow strip lights from both sides, behind product, 
light travels through glass, internal refraction visible, 
backlit effect through product, controlled caustic reflections.
```

**Metal / highly reflective product**:
```
Controlled reflection environment: large white diffusion panels reflected 
in surface, one bright directional specular from [DIRECTION], 
long highlight running [AXIS]. Black negative fill from [DIRECTION] 
to create contrast edge. Reflection shows abstract clean environment, 
not actual room.
```

**Matte product**:
```
Soft even illumination, large light sources close to subject, 
no specular highlights visible, even shadow graduation, 
surface texture revealed by light direction.
```

**Skincare / beauty product with liquid**:
```
Backlight from directly behind product, [KELVIN]K, 
visible glow through bottle if transparent, 
front fill from large soft source, minimal contrast.
```

## Section 5: Lens and Camera for Product

### Focal Length Choices

| Focal Length | Effect | Best For |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| 50mm | Natural perspective | General packshot |
| 85mm | Slight compression, natural product shape | Hero shots, premium |
| 100mm macro | Neutral perspective at close range, maximum detail | Texture shots, detail |
| 150–200mm | Strong compression, very flat perspective | Label photography, arrangement shots |

### Camera Height

- **Eye level** (parallel to product midpoint): neutral, catalog standard
- **Slightly above** (15–20°): clean, commercial, most e-commerce
- **Low angle** (below product midpoint, looking up): dramatic, heroic, premium
- **45° overhead**: lifestyle, editorial, flat lay

### Depth of Field

- **f/8–f/16**: Everything in frame sharp, full product detail — standard for packshot
- **f/4–f/5.6**: Subject sharp, background softly blurred — hero shots, premium
- **f/2.0–f/2.8**: Very shallow DOF, selective focus on one element — creative editorial, detail shots

---

## Section 6: Shadow Treatment

Shadow is a creative element that communicates material weight, surface interaction, and spatial grounding.

| Shadow Type | When to Use |
|-------------|-------------|
| No shadow | E-commerce pure white, maximum background-removal compatibility |
| Soft drop shadow | Clean white packshot where a slight shadow adds depth |
| Natural cast shadow | Lifestyle, product-in-context, premium editorial |
| Reflected shadow (on glossy surface) | When product sits on mirror or high-gloss surface |
| Hard graphic shadow | Graphic design aesthetic, high-contrast editorial |

## Negative Prompt (Product)

```
blurry, low quality, jpeg artifacts, oversaturated, color cast, 
wrong product shape, floating object, unrealistic materials, 
visible studio equipment, person in frame (unless brief requires), 
text errors, distorted label, warped perspective, lens flare (unless intentional)
```

## Platform Notes

**Flux**: Best photorealistic product renderer. Use `flux-pro` or `flux-ultra` for maximum material fidelity. Does not handle text on labels reliably — inpaint or composite labels separately.

**Ideogram**: Use when the product label with text is essential to the image. Ideogram's text rendering is superior for label photography.

**Midjourney**: Use for styled, editorial product with artistic quality. Less reliable for pure white packshot accuracy.

**Magnific upscale**: Run all hero product images through Magnific creative upscale to recover material texture and surface detail.
