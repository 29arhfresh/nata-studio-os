# Template — Portrait

Use this template for headshots, character portraits, beauty images, lifestyle portraits, and editorial people photography. This template covers any image where a human being is the primary subject.

---

## When to Use This Template

- Executive headshots and professional portraits
- Beauty and skincare campaigns
- Celebrity and editorial portraiture
- Lifestyle portraits for brand campaigns
- Social content featuring a person
- Character studies and conceptual portraiture

---

## Portrait Prompt Structure

```
[SUBJECT DESCRIPTION] + [POSE AND EXPRESSION] + [ENVIRONMENT] + [LIGHTING] + [LENS AND FRAME] + [SKIN AND TEXTURE] + [COLOR GRADE] + [PRODUCTION CUES]
```

---

## Section 1: Subject Description

Describe the person with enough specificity that the model cannot substitute a generic figure.

### Required Elements

| Element | Guidance | Example |
|---------|----------|---------|
| Age range | 5–10 year range, not a specific age | "late 30s," "mid 50s," "early 20s" |
| Gender presentation | State clearly | "woman," "man," "non-binary person" |
| Skin tone | Descriptive adjective + tone | "warm deep brown," "light cool beige," "rich warm ebony" |
| Hair | Color + length + style + texture | "natural black coils, shoulder-length, loose" |
| Eyes | Color + notable shape | "pale grey-blue, slightly downturned corners" |
| Distinctive features | Freckles, birthmarks, glasses, scars — specifics improve consistency | "light freckles across nose and cheeks" |
| Wardrobe | Garment + color + fit + material | "fitted navy merino turtleneck" |

### Subject Template

```
[AGE RANGE]-year-old [GENDER PRESENTATION], [SKIN TONE AND TEXTURE] skin, 
[HAIR DESCRIPTION — color, length, style, texture], [EYE DESCRIPTION], 
[DISTINCTIVE FEATURES if any], wearing [WARDROBE DESCRIPTION].
```

---

## Section 2: Pose and Expression

State pose and expression explicitly. Models default to a generic standing, forward-facing, neutral expression without direction.

### Pose Language

**Head position**: direct gaze, three-quarter profile, full profile, looking down, looking up, looking to the left, eyes closed

**Body position**: seated, standing, leaning, walking, reclining

**Arms and hands**: hands in lap, one hand touching face, arms crossed, hands behind back, holding an object

**Weight and stance**: weight on left hip, shoulders dropped and relaxed, slight lean forward, back straight

### Expression Language

**Range**: slight smile, closed-mouth smile, full smile, neutral, focused, contemplative, amused, serious, laughing

**Eye quality**: engaged, warm, distant, alert, soft, direct

### Pose Template

```
[POSE DESCRIPTION — body position, stance], [HEAD POSITION], 
[HAND AND ARM POSITION], [EXPRESSION], [EYE QUALITY].
```

## Section 3: Environment

### Studio Portrait

```
[BACKGROUND TYPE AND COLOR], [GRADIENT OR TEXTURE DETAIL], 
[VISIBLE FLOOR — yes/no, color], [PROP — if any].
```

**Background options**:
- `seamless white cyclorama, clean floor-wall junction`
- `seamless mid-grey backdrop, slight vignette`
- `seamless black background, no visible texture`
- `warm charcoal grey seamless, very slight warm gradient`
- `blurred architectural space, [color] walls, out of focus`

### Environmental Portrait

```
[LOCATION TYPE], [SPECIFIC ENVIRONMENTAL ELEMENTS], [TIME OF DAY], 
[WEATHER if exterior], [DEPTH FROM BACKGROUND — how far behind subject].
```

---

## Section 4: Lighting

Lighting is the most important technical specification in a portrait. Do not abbreviate this section.

### Studio Lighting Setups

**Single source — dramatic**:
```
Single large softbox at camera [left/right], 45° down, [Kelvin]K, 
no fill, shadow side dark, slight rim light from behind at camera [right/left].
```

**Two-source — balanced**:
```
Large octabox key light at camera left, 45° down, [Kelvin]K. 
Silver reflector fill at camera right, 1:3 fill ratio. 
No rim light. White background lit separately.
```

**Three-point — commercial**:
```
Large softbox key at camera left, 45° down, 5500K. 
Medium softbox fill at camera right, lower intensity, 1:2 ratio. 
Hair light from above and behind, camera right. Background light on 
white seamless, even exposure.
```

**Named setups**:
- `Rembrandt lighting — key light 45° from side and above, small triangle of light on shadow cheek`
- `Butterfly / paramount — key light directly above and slightly in front, symmetrical shadow under nose`
- `Split lighting — key light exactly 90° to side, half face in shadow`
- `Loop lighting — key light 30° to side and 45° above, small loop shadow from nose`

### Natural Light

```
[DIRECTION] window light, [QUALITY — soft/hard/diffused], [Kelvin]K, 
[FILL — reflector/ambient/none], [BACK LIGHT OR BACKLIGHT — present/absent].
```

## Section 5: Lens and Frame

### Focal Length Guide

| Focal Length | Effect | Best For |
|-------------|--------|---------|
| 35mm | Slight environmental context, mild distortion | Environmental portraits, editorial |
| 50mm | Neutral perspective, natural proportions | Lifestyle, documentary |
| 85mm | Slight compression, flattering for faces | Standard portrait, beauty |
| 135mm | Strong compression, face flattening, background isolation | Headshots, celebrity portraiture |
| 200mm | Maximum compression, very short DOF, strong background isolation | Beauty close-ups, magazine covers |

### Aperture and Depth of Field

| Aperture | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| f/1.2–f/1.8 | Very shallow DOF, strong bokeh, tiny focus plane |
| f/2.0–f/2.8 | Shallow DOF, soft bokeh, eyes sharp, ears slightly soft |
| f/4.0–f/5.6 | Moderate DOF, subject sharp, background softly blurred |
| f/8–f/11 | Deep focus, subject and near background sharp |

### Shot Size Reference

| Shot Size | Frame Includes |
|-----------|---------------|
| ECU | Eyes and nose only |
| CU | Chin to top of head |
| MCU | Collarbone to top of head |
| MS | Waist to top of head |
| MWS | Thighs to top of head |
| WS | Full body with space above and below |

### Lens Template

```
[FOCAL LENGTH]mm portrait lens equivalent, f/[APERTURE], 
[SHOT SIZE] framing, camera at [EYE LEVEL / slightly below / slightly above], 
subject on [center / left third / right third].
```

## Section 6: Skin and Hair Quality

These descriptors significantly improve the realism of skin and hair rendering.

### Skin Descriptors

**Natural and realistic**:
`natural skin texture, fine pores visible, subsurface scattering warmth, 
healthy without airbrushed perfection, light surface luminosity`

**Beauty / commercial (more polished)**:
`smooth even-toned skin, professional retouching quality, luminous highlight 
on forehead and cheekbones, no blemishes visible`

**Textural / raw**:
`highly detailed skin texture, visible pores, fine lines present, 
natural imperfections retained, documentary portrait quality`

### Hair Descriptors

**Natural, dry**:
`natural hair texture, individual strands visible, matte finish, slight flyaways at crown`

**Polished**:
`smooth styled hair, clean edges, slight natural shine, professional blowout quality`

**Textured / curly**:
`defined curl pattern, natural frizz at edges, volumetric shape, individual 
curl springs visible`

## Section 7: Color Grade

```
Color grade: [OVERALL TEMPERATURE — warm/cool/neutral], 
[SHADOW TREATMENT — crushed/lifted/natural], 
[HIGHLIGHT TREATMENT — compressed/open/blown], 
[SKIN TONE TREATMENT — leads/neutral/cool].
Reference: [FILM STOCK / PHOTOGRAPHER / COLOR HOUSE].
```

### Common Portrait Grades

**Natural editorial**: `warm natural, lifted shadow floor, compressed highlights, skin tones warm and leading, Kodak Portra 400 reference`

**High-key commercial**: `clean white, no color cast, bright even exposure, skin tones neutral pink, Nike campaign aesthetic`

**Dramatic low-key**: `crushed black shadows, lifted highlights, high contrast, cool shadow color, warm highlight color, cinematic portrait`

**Cool editorial**: `cool desaturated grade, blue-grey shadows, neutral highlights, skin tones slightly cool, Vogue Paris aesthetic`

## Section 8: Production Quality Cues

Select the quality anchors appropriate to the output type:

**Beauty / skincare**:
```

shot on Phase One, ultra-high resolution, tack sharp focus on eyes
```

**Executive headshot**:
```
corporate headshot photography, professional quality, clean background, 
sharp and confident, business portrait, shot on Canon 5D Mark IV
```

**Editorial portrait**:
```
editorial photography, published in [MAGAZINE NAME], 
art directed, natural light portraiture, film-inspired quality
```

**Lifestyle brand**:
```
lifestyle photography, brand campaign quality, warm and approachable, 
natural and real, not over-produced
```

## Negative Prompt (Portrait)

```
blurry, low quality, jpeg artifacts, oversaturated, overexposed, 
deformed hands, extra fingers, missing fingers, distorted face, 
asymmetrical eyes, crossed eyes, uneven eye size, uncanny valley, 
plastic skin, overly retouched, artificial lighting, blown highlights, 
bad anatomy, poorly drawn face, watermark, text overlay
```
